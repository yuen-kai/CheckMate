CheckMate

CheckMate is an app created by Yuen Kai Chow which can help boost your productivity.

Receive the benefits of a schedule without all the hassle. Easily create an optimized, adjusting schedule that fits into your day. Get your work done on time. It's all one click away!

Features:

Simple to use - To make a schedule, simply enter the tasks you need to complete and the times you’re available to work.

Optimized Schedule - Using a complex mathematical algorithm, CheckMate will create an optimized schedule so you can finish tasks on time.

Automatic Rescheduling - Start, pause, and finish tasks at your own pace! CheckMate will reschedule to follow your workflow.

Pacing - In addition to the schedule, CheckMate will show you how much extra time you have or how much more time is needed to complete all your tasks.

Saved Tasks - Don’t have time to finish a task? It will be saved for you to do next time!
