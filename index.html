About CheckMate


CheckMate is a free time blocking and daily planner app, which helps create schedules for you. No more procrastinating. No more trouble editing your entire agenda when something comes up. No more effort organizing your schedule. CheckMate handles all the hassle for you.








How Does It Work?


Time blocking is a time management and productivity technique where a period of time is divided into blocks for specific tasks. Organized schedules with this technique are useful to keep track of your day and stop procrastination. It improves your focus and motivation. No more “I’ll do it later”; When you can see your time laid out right in front of you, it’s a lot harder to procrastinate.





Why Use CheckMate?

CheckMate uses this technique of time blocking and handles the hassle for you.


Easy: There's no need to think about when you want to do a certain task; All you need is some basic information and CheckMate will do the rest.

Fast: Tasks and events can be set for multiple days, letting you get into working faster. Also, events from your device’s calendar can easily be added into your schedule.

Optimized: Using a complex mathematical algorithm, CheckMate creates an optimized schedule so you can work most effectively.

Flexible: Don’t worry if something suddenly comes up or you underestimate or overestimate the length of a task. Start, pause, finish, or edit tasks whenever you need to; Your schedule will automatically adjust for you.

Pacing: In addition to making a schedule, CheckMate will show you how much more time is needed to complete the current task as well as all of your tasks. There are also notification reminders to keep you on track.

Saved Tasks: Don’t have time to finish a task? Don't worry - CheckMate will save it for you to do next time!
