About CheckMate


CheckMate is a free productivity app developed by Yuen Kai Chow, which helps create adjusting, optimized schedules for you. No more trouble editing your entire agenda when something comes up. No more effort organizing your schedule. CheckMate handles all the hassle for you.





Features:


Easy: To make a schedule, simply enter the tasks you need to complete and the times you’re available to work (work times).

Fast: Tasks and work times can be set for multiple days, letting you get into working faster.

Optimized: Using a complex mathematical algorithm, CheckMate creates an optimized schedule so you can work most effectively.

Flexible: Start, pause, and finish tasks at your own pace. You can even easily edit your tasks! CheckMate will reschedule to follow your workflow.

Pacing: In addition to making a schedule, CheckMate will show you how much extra time you have or how much more time is needed to complete all of your tasks.

Saved Tasks: Don’t have time to finish a task? Don't worry - CheckMate will save it for you to do next time!




Why use CheckMate?


Schedules improve productivity. However, unlike traditional ones, CheckMate's agendas are better in a variety of ways:

They are easily editable.

They keep track of your recurring tasks and work times so you don't have to enter them every time.

They are very flexible, allowing you to work at your own pace and on whatever task you want.

They help you keep your pacing.

It automatically prioritizes important tasks so you complete them on time.
