CheckMate

Receive the benefits of a schedule without all the hassle. Features: Simple to use - To make a schedule, simply enter the tasks you need to complete and the times you’re available to work. Optimized Schedule - Using a complex mathematical algorithm, CheckMate will create an optimized schedule so you can finish tasks on time. Automatic Rescheduling - Start, pause, and finish tasks at your own pace! CheckMate will reschedule to follow your workflow. Pacing - In addition to the schedule, CheckMate will show you how much extra time you have or how much more time is needed to complete all your tasks. Saved Tasks - Don’t have time to finish a task? It will be saved for you to do next time!
